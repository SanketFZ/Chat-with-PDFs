# Chat with PDFs

## Asks your pdfs what are they and whats in them ??

## Tech Stack used
### Streamlit
### Google gemini 
### Langchain - FAISS
### Langchain - embeddings


## Steps to use
1.  Upload Your PDF
2.  Click Submit and Process
3.  Ask your Questions to PDFs
4.  If it doesn't answer wait it will ask te  gemini LLM to respond
